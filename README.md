ResQEd is a comprehensive safety and disaster-readiness platform designed to strengthen awareness, preparedness, and response capabilities within educational institutions. Our goal is simple yet powerful: educate, empower, and protect.

Key Features of ResQEd:

 1. Learning & Awareness Module-
Interactive digital content, animations, videos, quizzes, and gamified lessons
Covers fire safety, earthquake response, floods, and more
Accessible in multiple languages for inclusive learning

2. Simulation & Hands-On Training-
AR/VR-based virtual simulations for immersive safety scenarios
Drill scheduler for planned fire/earthquake mock drills
Role-based training for students, teachers, and staff

3. Emergency Response System-
One-Tap SOS linking to nearby police, fire stations, and hospitals
Real-time alerts and instructions during emergencies
Offline functionality to ensure reliability even without network access

4. First Aid & Basic Rescue Training-
Step-by-step tutorials for essential first-aid skills
AI-based query assistance

5. Administration Dashboard-
Track training completion and participation
Detailed reports for students and staff
Database of emergency contacts and local authorities

This interface shows the Student Dashboard, built for accessibility and easy learning.
The system also includes an Administrative Dashboard to track training progress and monitor activities, and a Staff Dashboard to support both students and management.
